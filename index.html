About IDR

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute focused on digital, cyber and technology risk roles in regulated environments.

Through collaboration with UK universities and industry partners, IDR bridges academic insight with real-world risk management projects.

Our Model

Academy

Bootcamps and structured programs to train the next generation of digital risk professionals.

Innovation

Developing future risk frameworks, AI governance models and emerging technology research.

Advisory

Helping organisations implement frameworks such as NIST, ISO 27001 and NIS2.

Who We Serve

IDR supports students, early-career professionals and practitioners working in sectors such as financial services and critical infrastructure seeking upskilling in digital risk and cyber governance.
